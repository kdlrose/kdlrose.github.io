hackstrology

free and mostly open-source software solutions for working astrologers compiled by @sailorfe 👩🏻‍💻. while you're here, why not book a reading with me or check out my newsletter?

🌟 tested

  • Astrodienst, the best free online chart generator
  • Astrolog, CLI chart generator in C++
  • astrokeys, QMK unicode map for astrological glyphs (mine 💅🏻)
  • AstroMatrix, Android app (paywalled)
  • TimeNomad, iPhone/iPad app (paywalled)

🔭 watching

  • Skylendar, a GUI using Qt5 and swisseph as a PostgreSQL database
